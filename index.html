Meet Luka Lukic, a 22-year-old sophomore at UCR who has established himself as a professional logo maker for over 4 years. Originally from Serbia, Luka moved to the United States with his family at a young age. Growing up, he always had a passion for creativity and enjoyed tinkering with art and design. However, it wasn't until high school when Luka discovered his talent for logo design.

Inspired by the logos he saw on his favorite products, Luka began experimenting with different software and techniques to create his own designs. He quickly realized that he had a natural talent for it and began taking on logo design projects for family and friends. As he honed his skills, Luka started to attract more clients and soon turned his passion into a full-time job. He began working with local businesses and start-ups, creating logos that helped them establish their brand identity and stand out in their respective markets. Luka's eye for design and attention to detail have earned him a reputation as one of the best logo designers in the area. His clients rave about his ability to capture the essence of their brand and create logos that are both visually striking and memorable. Despite his success as a logo maker, Luka is committed to continuing his education and pursuing his passion for design. As a sophomore at UCR, he's studying Economics and is constantly looking for new ways to push the boundaries of his creativity. Outside of work and school, Luka enjoys spending time with friends and family, listening to music, and exploring the outdoors. He's also an avid traveler and has visited several countries, drawing inspiration from the cultures and landscapes he encounters. With his talent and dedication to his craft, there's no doubt that Luka Lukic has a bright future ahead of him in the world of design. Whether he's creating logos for small businesses or working on his own projects, Luka's passion for design shines through in everything he does.
